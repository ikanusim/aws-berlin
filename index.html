The Hack

Build a working demo of something cool with AWS. This can be anything you want, e.g. a web or mobile app, interactive UI, or big data tool.

The Rules

  • Form a team of 2 - 4 hackers. (You can do this on site)
  • Each team has 20 hours to go from idea to working code.
  • All teams must present a 3-minute working demo on Saturday.
  • Anarchy rules – No limit on API, language or ideas. Come up with something awe-inspiring!

What to bring

  • A personal laptop.
  • There will be constant snacks n’ drinks available. Amazon will do a pizza run on Friday evening and provide breakfast Saturday morning.
  • However bring a couple of Euros for ice cream and lunch on Friday; this is going to be a hard day’s night.
  • Do your best boy/girl scout impression and bring some spare clothes alongside a spruce kit, or at least a stick of deodorant and some gum. Old school pens and a pad might not go amiss either.
  • Some music to code to and a machine to play it. Alternatively, headphones to cut out ambient music/noise.
  • Ideas! Brains will be stormed on the day, but think about what lights your fire – remember the aim is to build a working demo of something cool with AWS.

  • 2nd Place: A Kindle for each member of your team
